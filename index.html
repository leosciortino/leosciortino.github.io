About Me

I am a first year PhD student in Computer Science at University of Utah, advised by Prof. Mary Hall and Prof. Saday Sadayappan. I am interested in Compiler Optimization, Programming Languages, Computer Architecture, and their intersection. I graduated from the University of Rochester in 2025 with a BS in Computer Science and BA in Mathematics where I did research under Prof. Chen Ding.
